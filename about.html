About E.A.D Interim Agency

Building partnerships that drive success since 2007

Our Story

Founded in 2007 as a subsidiary of Halt Group, E.A.D Interim Agency was born from a vision to transform the manpower supply industry in Djibouti. We recognized a critical need for a reliable, professional staffing partner that truly understood both the demands of construction and industrial projects and the local labor market.

Over the years, we have grown from a small operation to become Djibouti's premier manpower supply agency. Our success is built on a foundation of trust, quality, and an unwavering commitment to matching the right workers with the right projects.

Today, we proudly serve over 50 companies across construction, industrial, logistics, and hospitality sectors, having successfully placed more than 500 skilled workers on projects that shape Djibouti's future.
