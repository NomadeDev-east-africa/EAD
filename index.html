About E.A.D Interim Agency

E.A.D Interim Agency, a subsidiary of Halt Group, is Djibouti's premier manpower supply agency specializing in construction and industrial sectors.

Since 2007, we have been connecting skilled professionals with leading companies, providing reliable staffing solutions that drive project success.

Our deep understanding of the local labor market, combined with rigorous screening processes, ensures that we match the right workers with the right projects every time.
